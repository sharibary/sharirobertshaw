Decades

My team was tasked with creating a video for State Farn Bank on how to save money for retirement. We decided to show how over time a dollar does not grow sitting at home. I art directed and helped produced this stop motion video which was played on iPads in Agent offices and won a Silver Addey Award in 2012.
