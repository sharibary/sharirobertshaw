Next Door

State Farm opened a co-working space and public cafe in the neighborhood of Lincoln Park in Chicago. They offer free financial coaching and classes to anyone in the community. The space is hugely popular and my team designed work for their initiatives, print materials for their classes, and also worked on signs to help customers understand the space and what they offer.
