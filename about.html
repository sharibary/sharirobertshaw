Hi I'm Shari, an American digital and print designer living in Bath UK. I am experienced in branding design, print design, responsive web design, UI and UX for e-commerce, and designing marketing materials and advertisments. I have learned just enough code to build this site.

I currently work at Omobono as a Creative Digital Designer where I design beautiful digital experiences for big brands. I previously worked at Meanbee designing e-commerce websites, and at Bath College as a Publishing lecturer in their Media department. In America, I worked for State Farm in their in-house design department for 7 years.

I have a love for typography and taught a Graphic Design class focused on type at Illinois State University.

In my spare time I also volunteer as IT and Commmunication director for Bath Welcomes Refugees and am very passionate about helping during the refugee crisis going on in the world at the moment.

I am currently accepting freelance work, if you are interested in my help, have questions, or just want to say hi, contact me at shari.robertshaw@gmail.com
